Our Motto

    Endorce

    Donate to Stella and Rosie's Cat Rescue!

    We are a nonprofit organization who rely on donations to keep our doors open. By endorcing Stella and Rosie's Cat Rescue, you are helping cats find forever homes and keeping them off of the streets.

    Embrace

    Adopt a cat who needs a loving home!

    Stella and Rosie's Cat Rescue is focused on finding homes for cats only. Therefore, you can be sure our cats are recieving the best care and resources.

    Enpower

    Help your cat be the best family member the they can be!

    With our exclusive state of the art cat training facilities, you can ensure that your "not so polite kitty" will learn cat etiquette to become a well behaved member of the family.

    Educate

    Create awareness in the local community!

    We are dedicated to educating the community about homeless cats. We help you to understand the responsibilites of adopting a cat and the joys of adding them to your family.
