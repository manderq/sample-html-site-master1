History

Stella and Rosieâ€™s Cat Rescue Center was inspired by our founders two cats, Stella and Rosie, who were previously held at a local rescue center. When searching for cats online and at local humane societies, our founder was shocked by the number of cats who did not have homes. She found that many of these cats were not desirable, to some people, because of training issues. From that moment on, she knew she wanted to create a rescue center focused solely on cat rescue and rehabilitation. Our Mission

    Endorse

    Donate to Stella and Rosie's Cat Rescue!

    We are a nonprofit organization who rely on donations to keep our doors open. By endorcing Stella and Rosie's Cat Rescue, you are helping cats find forever homes and keeping them off of the streets.

    Embrace

    Adopt a cat who needs a loving home!

    Stella and Rosie's Cat Rescue is focused on finding homes for cats only. Therefore, you can be sure our cats are recieving the best care and resources.

    Empower

    Help your cat be the best family member the they can be!

    With our exclusive state of the art cat training facilities, you can be sure that your "not so polite kitty" will learn cat etiquette to become a well behaved member of the family.

    Educate

    Create awareness in the local community!

    We are dedicated to educating the community about homeless cats. We help you to understand the responsibilities of adopting a cat and the joys of adding them to your family.

Policy

Top To Bottom Cat PLAY ZONE

The floors to the ceiling are designed for cat play and climbing.

100% Cage-less

All cats are trained to enjoy the company of other cats.

No Kill

No cats are killed for any reason, ever.

24/7 Care Staff

Cats are never left alone and are watched day and night.

Affordable

Adoptable cats services are cost friendly, and prices are never increased based on demand.

Experienced Staff

All staff members are cat aficionados.
